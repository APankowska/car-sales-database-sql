# SQL Queries

## SQL statements necessary to satisfy the following queries:
- **Query 1**: Produce a list of customers in customer number order - ['Q1.sql'](Q1.sql)
- **Query 2**: Produce a list of sales staff ordered by centre number then salesperson number -['Q2.sql'](Q2.sql)
- **Query 3**: Produce a list of car sales ordered by salesperson number then car price -['Q3.sql'](Q3.sql)
- **Query 4**: Produce output which lists which outputs the number of cars sold by each salesperson for the past 6 months -['Q4.sql'](Q4.sql)
- **Query 5a**: Produce output which lists of the total cost of the cars sold by each salesman in the past month - ['Q5a.sql'](Q5.sql)
- **Query 5b**: Add the commission earned by each salesman to the above (5a) report -['Q5b.sql'](Q5B.sql)
- **Query 6**:  Produce output which lists details of the most expensive car sold in the past month -['Q6.sql'](Q6.sql)
- **Query 7**:  Produce output which lists of all car sales in the past month. Include salesperson number, salesperson name, customer name and customer addressin your output. Order by salesperson number -['Q7.sql'](Q7.sql)
- **Query 8**:  Produce a list of customers that do not currentlyhave any car sales in the car sale table -['Q8.sql'](Q8.sql)
